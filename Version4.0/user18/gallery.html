Some of Oyasumi Punpuns characters!

Hover over the images to get the answer

1

Punpun Onodera

Punpun Onodera (小野寺 プンプン, Onodera Punpun) is the main character of Oyasumi Punpun. He belongs to a dysfunctional family.

2

Aiko Tanaka

In elementary school, Aiko is an outgoing and fearless child. She has an affinity for escapism and pretending and intimidates Punpun with her often times extreme demands of their love.

3

Masumi Seki

Masumi Seki is one of Punpun's childhood friends and is best friends with Shimizu. He has a rough personality and a phobia of fire.

4

God

Afro God is the overarching antagonist, he is Punpun's personal God, whom is summoned by the chant Punpun's uncle taught him, "Dear God, dear God, tinkle tinkle hoy!" He is gradually revealed to be a manifestation of Punpun's darker thoughts.
