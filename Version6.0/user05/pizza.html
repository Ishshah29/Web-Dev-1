Pizza Recipes

1. Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Tear the mozzarella into chunks and scatter it over the sauce.
  5. Add a pinch of salt, and drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, and add fresh basil leaves on top before serving.

2. Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 30-40 slices of pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to fit your pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange pepperoni slices over the cheese.
  6. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil around the edges of the crust.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  8. Slice and serve hot!

3. Vegetarian Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup red onions (sliced)
  • Black olives, sliced

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll the pizza dough onto a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Top with bell peppers, mushrooms, red onions, and black olives.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!

4. BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 8 oz cooked chicken breast (shredded)
  • 1/2 red onion (sliced thinly)
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a floured surface.
  3. Spread BBQ sauce over the dough.
  4. Layer shredded chicken and red onion on top.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the chicken.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese melts and the crust is golden.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve!
