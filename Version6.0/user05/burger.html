Burger Recipes

1. Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 ratio)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the patties on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for about 1 minute.
  6. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, and adding lettuce, tomato, pickles, and condiments if desired. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. BBQ Bacon Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 ratio)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • Lettuce and onion (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  4. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of grilling to melt.
  5. Toast the buns on the grill for 1 minute.
  6. Assemble the burger by spreading BBQ sauce on the bottom bun. Add the cheeseburger patty, bacon, lettuce, and onion if desired. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Veggie Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and mashed
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat hamburger buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, avocado, cheese

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the mashed black beans, breadcrumbs, grated carrots, chopped onions, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and heated through.
  4. Toast the whole wheat buns for 1 minute in the skillet or in the oven.
  5. Assemble the veggie burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, and topping with your choice of lettuce, tomato, avocado, and cheese.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

4. Spicy Jalapeno Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 ratio)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices pepper jack cheese
  • 4 fresh jalapenos, sliced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce and tomato (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
  4. During the last minute of grilling, place a slice of pepper jack cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for about 1 minute.
  6. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise and sriracha sauce to create a spicy mayo sauce.
  7. Assemble the burger by spreading the spicy mayo on the bottom bun, then adding the cheeseburger patty, sliced jalapenos, lettuce, and tomato. Top with the other half of the bun.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
