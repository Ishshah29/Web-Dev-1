Spaghetti Recipes

1. Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a large pan, heat a bit of olive oil over medium heat and cook the onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, red wine (if using), tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

2. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook until the garlic is golden brown (about 2-3 minutes).
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat. Add a little reserved pasta water if the pasta seems dry.
  4. Stir in the fresh parsley and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

3. Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 8 oz pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, cook the pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the pan from heat and add the minced garlic, cooking for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with pancetta and toss to coat in the bacon fat.
  5. Slowly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce. If the pasta seems too dry, add a bit of reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Serve immediately.

4. Spaghetti with Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a food processor, blend the basil, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  3. While the food processor is running, slowly add the olive oil until the pesto reaches a smooth, spreadable consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the pesto sauce, adding reserved pasta water if needed to thin the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
