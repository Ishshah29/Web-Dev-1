About Bugatti

Founded by Ettore Bugatti in 1909, the company quickly became synonymous with performance and luxury. The first car, the Bugatti Type 13, was a game-changer, and the brand has continued to lead the industry ever since.

Bugatti's legacy is built on the foundation of excellence in design, engineering, and innovation. Today, the brand is known for its world-record-breaking performance and breathtaking designs.

Bugatti Milestones

Bugatti's journey has been full of remarkable achievements and historic moments. Here are some key milestones that have defined the brand:

  • 1909: Founded by Ettore Bugatti in Molsheim, Alsace.
  • 1920s: The Type 35 became the most successful race car in history.
  • 2005: The Veyron 16.4, the world's fastest car, was unveiled.
  • 2016: The Chiron became the new flagship, with 1,479 horsepower.
  • 2020: Bugatti produced the Divo, focused on track precision and performance.
